Hola, I'm Iza Valle (pronounced like eza,but way better), an Ottawa based Graphic Designer and Illustrator I believe in creating—creating a story, an experience , and awesome solutions to help people perceive the world. Yes designs should be beautiful but far more than that, design  should have a purpose, a meaning—an action! There's always something to learn,  and there's always a solution to everything, limited only by how far you're willing to stretch the imagination. I’m way too curious and I enjoy finding the most elegant ways to harmonize content with form. That’s a fancy way of saying I take text and colours and images and I arrange them  in a way that immediately makes it usable for the user.
