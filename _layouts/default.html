Furevr

Furvr connects local rescue animals with loving forever homes. It is an animal rescue network that allows the user to browse adoptable pets and search based on their location, breed or age and view the profiles of animals in need of homes. Swipe through photos, favourite pets for later viewing, donate to local shelters, or share pets on social media to increase they’re chance of adoption. Furvr unites families with pets that compliment their lifestyles, allowing for maximum compatibility. Are you ready to meet your new best friend?

McMurray Aviation

McMurray Aviation specializies in charter flights to remote fishing and hunting camps, and transport goods to local native reserves. When creating the look and feel for McMurray Aviation, I wanted to reflect the ruggedness of their location and appeal to the target demographic. The overall look of the logo was greatly influenced by vintage badge design, and the boreal forest where the company is located. My goal was to design a logo the embodied the personality of both McMurray Aviation and the town of Fort McMurray itself.

Wild

This booklet contains quotes from a variety of nature enthusiasts, including John Muir and Henry David Thoreau. I found these quotes to be very inspiring and so I wanted to create a booklet which could bring these quotes to life, and visually communicate nature's beauty. Every quote was hand painted using watercolours, they were then scanned and touched up in Photoshop. I printed and bound the booklet myself.

TipJar

TipJar, an app for iOS, introduces a simple system that complies three different ratings to compile an appropriate tip, taking into account sales tax, service and quality. This app is very simple, using an easy rating system to collect the data needed for calculation. It can also be used for other services, such as a salon or taxi driver. Never second-guess your tip again!
