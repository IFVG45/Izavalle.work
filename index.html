Hey!

I'm Iza. I design, and draw and create all sorts of cool and interesting things. There's nothing I like more than creating something new and exciting. It's this curiosity that keeps me striving to invent and seek improvement in myself. If theres one thing I've learned as a graphic designer which is that if you aren't learning something new about the industry all the time, then you aren't growing as a designer.

I would like to be a major part of the graphic design development in ottawa. I get inspired by the other designers I'm surrounded with, in my school, and those I work with. I have a diverse background in Fine Arts, and years of experiance using a paintbrush. The brush I use now is digital, and my paint is pixels.
