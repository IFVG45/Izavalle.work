About Me

Hello my name is Iza (pronounced like eza,but way better).I believe in creating—creating a story, an experience , and awesome solutions to help people  perceive the world. Yes designs should be beautiful but far more than that, design  should have a purpose, a meaning—an action! There's always something to learn,  and there's always a solution to everything, limited only by how far you're willing  to stretch the imagination. I design things—websites, Illustration and prints  and animation. Design is what I do, but the thought behind the design,  the communication aspect, and how the user understands the design, is the reason I do it!.

I’m way too curious and I enjoy finding the most elegant ways to harmonize content with form. That’s a fancy way of saying I take text and colours and images and I arrange them in a way that immediately makes it usable for the user.So, let's stretch some brains and be extraordinary. You in?
